Local Coverage

Roofing & Siding Contractor Near You

Being local isn't just a talking point. It means we can get a crew to your house faster after a storm, and it means we already know how these homes are built and what kind of weather they're up against. We've worked on roofs in every town below, and we know the difference between a Wichita Falls hailstorm and a Lawton wind event.

Wichita Falls, TX

76301-76310

The largest city we serve, and the one that keeps us busiest. Older neighborhoods near Midwestern State University and homes lining Kell Blvd take a beating every spring when hail rolls through with the storm season.

Recent Job

Full roof replacement with Class 4 impact-resistant shingles after a spring hailstorm left a Wichita Falls roof with widespread bruising and cracked shingles.

Burkburnett, TX

76354

Our hometown. Just north of Wichita Falls near the Red River and Sheppard AFB, with a lot of military families who need work done right and done on schedule before the next PCS move.

Recent Job

Hail-damaged roof and gutter replacement for a Burkburnett homeowner, insurance-approved and completed in under two days.

Iowa Park, TX

76367

A tight-knit small town just west of Wichita Falls off US-287. Mostly ranch-style homes with low-slope rooflines that show wind and hail damage differently than the two-story houses in the city.

Recent Job

Siding and roof repair for an Iowa Park ranch home after a wind and hail event damaged the west-facing exterior.

Electra, TX

76360

Known as the Pump Jack Capital, with an older housing stock along the SH-25 corridor. Many of these roofs are original to the home, which means storm damage often uncovers years of wear underneath.

Recent Job

Storm damage roof repair and ridge vent replacement on an older Electra home after high winds tore loose several rows of shingles.

Vernon, TX

76384

The Wilbarger County seat off US-287. Vernon sees some of the widest temperature swings in our service area, which stresses shingles and flashing even before a storm shows up.

Recent Job

Full roof replacement and fascia repair for a Vernon homeowner after a hailstorm damaged the roof deck and trim.

Henrietta, TX

76365

The Clay County seat east of Wichita Falls, with a historic downtown and plenty of long-standing homes nearby that need a contractor who understands older roof construction.

Recent Job

Insurance-covered roof replacement for a Henrietta home after hail damage was confirmed during a free inspection.

Lawton, OK

73501-73507

The largest city we serve in Oklahoma, near Fort Sill, with fast-growing neighborhoods and a steady stream of new construction alongside older homes that still need repair work.

Recent Job

Hail damage roof and siding restoration for a Lawton homeowner, with the full insurance claim managed start to finish by our team.

Duncan, OK

73533

Known as the Buckle of the Wheat Belt, in Stephens County off US-81. Duncan has a lot of older brick homes with original roofs that have taken decades of Oklahoma weather.

Recent Job

Roof replacement with upgraded underlayment for a Duncan homeowner after severe hail compromised the existing roof system.
